Schattenspiel

Project

Hello everyone! We are a small team of three creative minds, and we’d like to introduce you to our animation project, "Schattenspiel".

The idea was born during our second semester and was brought to life over several months in the third and fourth semesters. Schattenspiel is a "low-poly" 3D animation that explores the of light and shadow – and challenges how easily our perception can be tricked. The inspiration for this project came from our team leader, Alexander Naumann, who was fascinated by a scene in "The Wizard of Oz": "A fearsome wizard, appearing powerful and grand—until a simple curtain is pulled aside, revealing revealing only a frail man behind the illusion." In our story, the main roles are taken by Caspar, a brave young boy, and Wolf, a little mouse. Together, they dive into an adventure where appearances are not what they seem.

Are you ready to uncover the truth? Step into the world of Schattenspiel and see for yourself!
